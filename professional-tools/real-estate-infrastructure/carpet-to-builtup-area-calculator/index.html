FAQ

What is the difference between carpet, built-up and super built-up area?

Carpet area is usable floor area; built-up adds wall thickness (~10%); super built-up adds a share of common areas via a loading factor.

What is a loading factor?

It is the percentage added to carpet area for common spaces like lobbies and stairs. Typical loading is 25–40%.

Which area should I compare?

RERA requires carpet area to be disclosed, so compare carpet area for a like-for-like price comparison.
