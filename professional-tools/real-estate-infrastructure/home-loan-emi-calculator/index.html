FAQ

How is home loan EMI calculated?

EMI = P × i × (1+i)^n ÷ ((1+i)^n − 1), where i is the monthly rate and n the number of months (years × 12).

How can I reduce my home loan interest?

A shorter tenure, part-prepayments or a lower interest rate all reduce total interest paid.

Does this include registration and stamp duty?

No. Those are one-time purchase costs and are separate from the loan EMI.
