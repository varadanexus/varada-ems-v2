Transporter Trip Profit Calculator

Calculate the real monthly profit from every truck, loaded trip and commodity after fuel, tolls, drivers, maintenance, permits, commission and other operating expenses.

1. Fleet and route

A round trip is one complete route cycle, including the return journey.

Fuel uses twice this distance for each round trip.

2. Commodities and freight revenue

Add every outbound or return-load commodity separately.

3. Direct and monthly costs

Select whether tolls and driver charges apply per trip, route cycle, truck or month.

4. Other expenses

Add loading, unloading, agent, bata, parking, repairs or any custom cost.

Important: Profit is only as exact as the inputs. Use actual route kilometres, loaded mileage, empty-return mileage where relevant, toll statements, driver terms, maintenance records, permits, insurance, finance costs and all cash expenses. GST and income tax are excluded because their treatment depends on the business and invoice structure.

How this improves the original spreadsheet

The spreadsheet model is preserved, including separate commodity revenue and route-cycle fuel calculations. This version supports any number of commodities and expenses, explicit toll and driver charge bases, maintenance per kilometre, fleet fixed costs, commission, per-trip profit, per-truck profit and break-even freight rate.
