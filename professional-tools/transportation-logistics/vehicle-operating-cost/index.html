FAQ

What is the average truck operating cost per km in India?

For a 10-tonne truck, operating cost ranges from ₹25–45/km depending on route, load factor and fuel price. Heavy trucks (25t+) cost ₹35–55/km.

What is the biggest cost in truck operations?

Fuel is typically 40–50% of total operating cost for a truck in India. Driver wages, tyres and maintenance together make up another 30–40%.

How is vehicle depreciation calculated?

Trucking companies typically use 15% WDV (Written Down Value) depreciation for taxation. For per-km costing, straight-line over 8–10 years is more useful.
