FAQ

What is an E-way bill?

An E-way bill is an electronic document generated on the GST portal for movement of goods worth more than ₹50,000. It is mandatory for interstate transport and for intrastate movement in most states.

What permits does a truck need for interstate transport?

For interstate goods transport: National Permit from the MV Department, RC, FC, insurance, PUC and driver's commercial licence. Goods requiring special handling (chemicals, food, pharma) may need additional permits.

What are the penalties for driving without a fitness certificate?

Driving without a valid FC attracts a fine and vehicle detention. For commercial vehicles, the fine can be up to ₹10,000. Repeat offences can lead to suspension of the vehicle permit.
