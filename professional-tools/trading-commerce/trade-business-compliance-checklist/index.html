FAQ

What is the penalty for non-filing of GST returns?

Late fee of ₹50/day (₹20/day for nil returns) up to ₹5,000 per return, plus 18% interest on tax liability. Consistent non-filing can lead to GST registration cancellation.

Is Shops Act registration mandatory for online trading businesses?

Most states require S&E registration for any business with even one employee. Online / e-commerce businesses operating from an office or warehouse need this registration.

When does a trading firm need a Statutory Audit?

Companies (all sizes) need CA audit. Proprietorships/partnerships need tax audit if turnover exceeds ₹1 crore (or ₹10 crore if 95%+ digital transactions). LLPs need audit if turnover > ₹40 lakh.
