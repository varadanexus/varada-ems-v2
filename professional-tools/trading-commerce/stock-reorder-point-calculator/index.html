FAQ

What happens if I reorder too late?

Stockouts mean lost sales, emergency procurement at high cost, and damaged customer relationships. Missed deliveries can cause contract penalties in B2B trading.

What is safety stock?

Safety stock is buffer inventory held to absorb demand spikes and supplier delays. It is the minimum stock you should never go below before placing the next order.

Should I use EOQ for all products?

EOQ works best for items with relatively stable demand. For high-value, slow-moving items use Just-In-Time. For highly seasonal items, use seasonal demand profiles instead.
