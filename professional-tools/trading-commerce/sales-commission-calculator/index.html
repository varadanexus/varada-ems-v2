FAQ

What TDS applies on commissions?

TDS under Section 194H is deducted at 5% on commission to agents (excluding employees) where annual payments exceed ₹15,000.

What is a typical agent commission in India?

Varies widely: consumer goods 2–5%, industrial products 3–8%, specialty products 5–15%, pharmaceutical/MedTech 5–10%. Higher margins allow higher commissions.

Should I structure a tiered commission plan?

Yes — tiered commissions drive growth by rewarding over-achievement. A base rate for standard volumes and a higher rate above target creates strong sales motivation.
