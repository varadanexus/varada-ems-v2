FAQ

What does a PR campaign cost in India?

A basic PR retainer in India starts at ₹50,000–1,00,000/month for small agencies. Mid-tier firms charge ₹1,50,000–3,00,000/month. For specific campaigns, budget ₹2–10 lakh total.

What is included in a PR agency retainer?

A retainer typically covers: media relations, press release writing and distribution, journalist pitching, social media monitoring, crisis communications support and monthly reporting.

How do I measure PR campaign success?

Key PR metrics: media coverage (clips), share of voice vs competitors, message pull-through rate, website traffic from PR, social shares and sentiment. Assign AVE (advertising value equivalent) for budget comparison.
