FAQ

What should an offer letter include?

An offer letter should state: designation, CTC structure, fixed vs variable split, joining date, probation period, notice period, work location, conditions (background check, educational verification) and offer expiry date.

Is an offer letter legally binding?

An offer letter is not a full employment contract but does create some legal obligations. An appointment letter (issued post-joining) is the formal employment agreement.

Can a company withdraw an offer letter?

Companies can legally withdraw offers before the candidate joins, though it can attract claims for expenses incurred (resignation, relocation). Best practice is to include an "offer subject to verification" clause.
