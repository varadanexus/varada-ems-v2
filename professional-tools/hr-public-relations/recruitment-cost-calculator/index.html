FAQ

What is a good cost per hire?

Average CPH in India varies significantly: ₹15,000–30,000 for junior roles, ₹50,000–1,50,000 for mid-level, ₹1,00,000–5,00,000+ for senior/C-suite positions.

How can I reduce cost per hire?

Employee referral programmes (low CPH, high retention), building an internal talent pool, using LinkedIn Recruiter efficiently and investing in employer branding reduce CPH significantly.

What is the recruiter agency fee percentage?

Standard agency fee in India is 8–12% of annual CTC for junior/mid roles, 15–20% for senior roles, and up to 25–33% for executive search.
