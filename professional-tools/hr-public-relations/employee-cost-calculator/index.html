FAQ

Why does an employee cost more than their CTC?

Beyond CTC, companies incur costs for office space, IT equipment, HR administration, training, recruitment, legal compliance and utilities. Total cost is typically 1.3–1.5× CTC.

What is the ESIC contribution?

ESIC (Employee State Insurance) applies if gross monthly salary ≤ ₹21,000. Employee contributes 0.75%; employer contributes 3.25% of gross wages.

How do I calculate true cost per employee?

Add CTC + employer PF + employer ESIC + office space + equipment + training + HR overhead + recruitment cost amortised over tenure.
