FAQ

What is the structural cost per sq ft?

Roughly ₹800–1,300 per sq ft for the RCC frame, depending on building height and loading.

What share of cost is the structure?

The structural frame is typically about a quarter to a third of total civil construction cost.

What drives structural cost up?

Height, spans, seismic zone, soil condition and heavy floor loads all increase steel and concrete quantities.
