FAQ

How much electricity does a hospital use per bed?

Typically 20–40 kWh per bed per day depending on HVAC load, imaging and climate.

How is monthly utility cost estimated?

Multiply per-bed daily usage by beds, the tariff and 30 days for each utility, then add them.

What drives hospital energy cost?

HVAC/air-conditioning, sterilisation, imaging equipment and 24×7 operation are the largest contributors.
