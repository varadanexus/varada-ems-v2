FAQ

What is hospital infection control?

Infection Prevention and Control (IPC) is the set of practices — hand hygiene, PPE, sterilisation, isolation, surveillance and waste management — that reduce healthcare-associated infections.

Why is hand hygiene critical?

Hand hygiene is the single most effective measure to prevent cross-transmission of infections in hospitals.

Does this replace a formal IPC audit?

No. It is a quick readiness indicator; formal accreditation requires assessment against the full NABH/ICMR standards.
