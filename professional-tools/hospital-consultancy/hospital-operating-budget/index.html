FAQ

What percentage of revenue goes to staff in a hospital?

Typically 35–45% of revenue; super-specialty hospitals with heavy specialist compensation can see staffing costs at 45–50%.

What is a typical hospital EBITDA margin?

Mature private hospitals in India typically operate at 18–28% EBITDA margin; new hospitals may be 5–15% for first 2–3 years.

How do I reduce hospital operating costs?

Vendor consolidation, drug inventory management, energy audits, shared services for non-clinical functions and technology for staff productivity are key levers.
