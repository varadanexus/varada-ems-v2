FAQ

What is NABL accreditation for hospitals?

NABL (under QCI) accredits medical testing laboratories to ISO 15189, assuring patients and regulators that test results are accurate and reliable.

How long does NABL accreditation take?

Typically 9–18 months from readiness to certificate, covering application, pre-assessment, assessment and any corrective actions.

What are the most common NABL audit failures?

Missing or outdated SOPs, gaps in equipment calibration records, absence of EQA participation and incomplete uncertainty of measurement calculations.
