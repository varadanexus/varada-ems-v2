FAQ

What does a hospital PMC consultant do?

A Project Management Consultant oversees DPR, architectural briefing, vendor selection, construction monitoring, equipment procurement, licensing and commissioning on behalf of the promoter.

How much does hospital consultancy cost in India?

Full-scope PMC typically costs 3–5% of project cost; for a ₹60 crore hospital, that is ₹1.8–3 crore in consultancy fees across 3 years.

When should I hire a hospital consultant?

Ideally at the concept stage — before land purchase — so the consultant can advise on location feasibility, clinical programme and regulatory requirements from the outset.
