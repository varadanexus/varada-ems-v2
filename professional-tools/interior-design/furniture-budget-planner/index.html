FAQ

What is the average furniture cost for a 3BHK in India?

A standard mid-range 3BHK furniture budget runs ₹4–7 lakh; premium ₹8–15 lakh; luxury ₹18 lakh and above, excluding modular kitchen.

Should I buy ready-made or custom furniture?

Custom furniture gives better space utilisation for irregular rooms; ready-made is faster and easier to replace. Many homeowners combine both.

Does this include mattresses and bed linen?

Furniture estimates cover structural pieces. Mattresses, curtains, cushions, rugs and linen are soft-furnishing budgets estimated separately.
