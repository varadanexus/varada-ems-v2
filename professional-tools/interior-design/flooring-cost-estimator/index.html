FAQ

What is the best flooring for Indian homes?

Vitrified tiles are the most popular for durability, low maintenance and cost. Marble is preferred for luxury homes; engineered wood for bedrooms where warmth is desired.

What is the labour cost for tile flooring in India?

Labour for tile laying runs ₹25–50 per sq ft depending on tile size and design complexity; large format tiles (80×80 cm and above) cost more to lay.

Why order 10% extra flooring?

Wastage from cuts, breakage during installation and matching future repairs means ordering 10–15% extra is standard practice.
