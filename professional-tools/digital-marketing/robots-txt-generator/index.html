FAQ

What is robots.txt?

robots.txt is a file at your domain root that tells search engine crawlers which pages or sections to crawl or avoid. It is a request, not a security measure.

Should I block /admin/ in robots.txt?

Yes — admin pages have no SEO value and blocking them conserves crawl budget for important content. Note: robots.txt does not secure admin pages — use authentication for that.

Does robots.txt affect SEO?

Blocking important pages from crawling will prevent them from ranking. Ensure key landing pages, blog and product pages are crawlable. Only block non-indexable paths.
