FAQ

What makes a perfect SEO score?

No single tool defines "perfect SEO" — focus on unique valuable content, fast page speed, quality backlinks, proper technical setup and mobile experience.

Which SEO factor matters most?

Content quality and relevance consistently rank as the top factor, followed by page experience (speed, mobile) and authoritative backlinks.

How do I improve my SEO score quickly?

Fix technical issues first (speed, mobile, HTTPS), then optimise title and meta tags, then improve content depth. Backlinks take longer but have high impact.
