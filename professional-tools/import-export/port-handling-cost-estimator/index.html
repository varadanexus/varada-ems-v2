FAQ

What is THC in port charges?

Terminal Handling Charges are fees levied by the port terminal for loading/unloading and moving containers. They are separate from ocean freight.

What is demurrage vs detention?

Demurrage is the charge for keeping a container inside the port terminal beyond the free period. Detention is for keeping it outside at your premises. Both can be ₹3,000–₹8,000/day.

How do I avoid demurrage charges?

Plan customs clearance before vessel arrival, submit import documents promptly, and choose ports with longer free periods for time-sensitive cargo.
