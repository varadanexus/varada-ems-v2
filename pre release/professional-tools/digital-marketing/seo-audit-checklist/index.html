FAQ

How often should I run an SEO audit?

A full technical and content audit quarterly; monitor crawl errors and page speed monthly via Google Search Console and PageSpeed Insights.

What are the most important SEO fixes?

Page speed on mobile, HTTPS, fixing crawl errors and ensuring unique title/meta tags are the highest-ROI fixes for most websites.

How long does SEO take to show results?

Technical fixes can show results in 2–6 weeks (faster crawling, error removal); content and link building take 3–12 months to impact rankings.
