FAQ

What is a good cost per lead in India?

CPL varies widely by industry: B2B SaaS ₹500–2,000; real estate ₹800–3,000; healthcare ₹300–1,200; education ₹200–800. Compare against customer lifetime value.

How do I reduce my cost per lead?

Improve landing page conversion rate (better headline, CTA, form), tighten audience targeting, use negative keywords for paid search, and A/B test ad creative.

What is the difference between a lead and a qualified lead?

A lead has shown interest (form fill, call); a qualified lead meets criteria like budget, authority and need (BANT) and is worth sales follow-up.
