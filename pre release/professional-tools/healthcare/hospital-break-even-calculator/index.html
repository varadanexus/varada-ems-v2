FAQ

What is ARPOB?

Average Revenue Per Occupied Bed-day — the average revenue a hospital earns for each occupied bed each day.

How is hospital break-even occupancy calculated?

Break-even bed-days = monthly fixed cost ÷ (ARPOB − variable cost per bed-day). Occupancy% = that ÷ (beds × 30) × 100.

What if break-even occupancy exceeds 100%?

It means the current pricing and cost structure cannot break even at full occupancy — ARPOB must rise or costs must fall.
