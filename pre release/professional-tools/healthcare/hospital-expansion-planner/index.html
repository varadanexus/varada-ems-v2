FAQ

How much does it cost to add hospital beds?

Multiply additional beds by an all-in per-bed cost (civil plus equipment), commonly ₹30–60 lakh per bed for multispecialty.

How many staff per bed does a hospital need?

Roughly 2–3 staff per bed across doctors, nurses and support for a multispecialty hospital running 24×7.

What else should an expansion plan cover?

Utilities, medical gas, licences, working capital and a ramp-up period before the new beds reach target occupancy.
