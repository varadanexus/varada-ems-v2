FAQ

How are hospital beds distributed across departments?

A typical multispecialty split allocates the most beds to general medicine and surgery, with ICU around 10–12% and the rest across specialties.

Can I change the ratios?

Yes — the split should reflect your case mix; a cardiac or maternity focus would shift beds accordingly.

Does this include day-care and OT?

No, it distributes inpatient beds only; day-care, OT tables and diagnostics are planned separately.
