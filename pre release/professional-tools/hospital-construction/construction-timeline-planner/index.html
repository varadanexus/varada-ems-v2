FAQ

How long does construction take?

Structure time ≈ area ÷ build speed; add design/approvals up front and finishing/commissioning at the end.

What build speed should I use?

It varies with labour, method and site; a few thousand sq ft per month is common for conventional RCC work.

What can delay a project?

Approvals, funding gaps, monsoon, material shortages and design changes are the usual causes.
