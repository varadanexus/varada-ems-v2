FAQ

What is CIF value?

CIF (Cost, Insurance, Freight) is the assessable value for customs duty. It includes the cost of goods, insurance and freight to the Indian port of entry.

What is Social Welfare Surcharge?

SWS is levied at 10% of the total customs duty (basic + other duties) under the Finance Act. It replaced the Education Cess from 2018.

Can IGST paid on imports be claimed as input tax credit?

Yes — IGST paid on imports is fully available as input tax credit (ITC) for registered GST businesses, as long as the goods are used for taxable supply.
