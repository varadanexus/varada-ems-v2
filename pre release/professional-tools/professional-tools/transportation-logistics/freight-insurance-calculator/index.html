FAQ

Is freight insurance mandatory?

No, it is not mandatory but strongly recommended. Most banks financing trade require marine insurance for L/C shipments. Carrier liability under Carriage of Goods Act is limited.

What does All Risk (ICC A) cargo insurance cover?

ICC A covers all physical loss or damage to cargo from any external cause, including water damage, theft, rough handling and breakage. Exclusions include inherent vice, delay and intentional damage.

How do I file a cargo insurance claim?

Immediately notify insurer (within 24 hours for visible damage). Get survey done by the insurer's surveyor. Document damage with photos, packing list and delivery note. File within the claim period (usually 60–90 days).
