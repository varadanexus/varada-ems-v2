FAQ

What is a good inventory turnover ratio?

Benchmarks vary by industry. FMCG/retail targets 6–12×; manufacturing 4–8×; engineering 2–4×. Higher is generally better — it means less capital tied up in stock.

What is Days Inventory Outstanding (DIO)?

DIO is the average number of days a company holds inventory before selling it. Lower DIO means faster-moving stock. DIO = 365 ÷ inventory turnover ratio.

How do I improve inventory turnover?

Improve demand forecasting, reduce safety stock where possible, implement ABC analysis (focus on fast movers), run promotions on slow movers, and negotiate shorter supplier lead times.
