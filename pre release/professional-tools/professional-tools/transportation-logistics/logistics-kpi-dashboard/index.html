FAQ

What is OTIF?

OTIF (On-Time In-Full) measures the percentage of orders delivered both on time AND in the correct quantity. It is the gold standard logistics KPI for supply chain reliability.

What is a good OTIF rate?

Best-in-class companies achieve 95%+ OTIF. 85–90% is average for most Indian logistics operations. Below 80% indicates significant supply chain reliability issues.

What is the Perfect Order Index?

Perfect Order Index measures orders that are on-time, in-full, damage-free and with correct documentation. It is a composite metric; achieving 90%+ across all dimensions is world-class.
