FAQ

What is the cost of a 6 ft wardrobe in India?

A 6×8 ft plywood wardrobe with sliding doors costs ₹55,000–90,000; MDF with swing doors ₹35,000–55,000; solid wood ₹1,20,000–2,00,000 depending on internal fittings.

Sliding vs swing wardrobe — which is better for small rooms?

Sliding wardrobes are better for rooms with limited floor space in front of the wardrobe; swing doors need 2–2.5 ft of clearance.

Should I choose plywood or MDF for a wardrobe?

Plywood is more durable and moisture-resistant, recommended in humid climates. MDF gives a smoother finish but is heavier and less moisture-tolerant.
