FAQ

Does interior design increase property value?

A well-executed premium interior can increase resale value by 10–20% for residential and 15–25% for commercial spaces, though market conditions dominate.

What is the ROI of renovating a rental property?

If interior investment increases monthly rent by ₹10,000 on a ₹15 lakh spend, payback is 12.5 years (rental basis); but property value uplift often makes total ROI positive in 5–7 years.

Does office interior affect productivity?

Research suggests a well-designed workplace (lighting, acoustics, ergonomics) can improve employee productivity by 5–15%, with measurable impact on retention and recruitment.
