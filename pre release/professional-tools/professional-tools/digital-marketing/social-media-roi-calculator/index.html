FAQ

How do I measure social media ROI?

ROI = (Revenue from social − total social spend) ÷ total social spend × 100. Use UTM tracking to attribute revenue to specific social campaigns.

Which social platform gives the best ROI in India?

For B2C, Instagram and Facebook typically deliver the best ROI; for B2B, LinkedIn generates higher-quality leads though at higher CPL; WhatsApp Business works well for re-engagement.

How long before social media shows ROI?

Paid social can show results within 2–4 weeks; organic social media ROI typically takes 6–12 months to compound as audience and authority build.
