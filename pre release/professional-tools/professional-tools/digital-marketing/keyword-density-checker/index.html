FAQ

What is the ideal keyword density for SEO?

Google does not publish an official ideal; 0.5–1.5% for the primary keyword is a widely accepted guideline. Focus on natural writing over exact density.

Does keyword density still matter for SEO?

Less so than a decade ago — Google's NLP understands context and synonyms. What matters more is covering the topic comprehensively with relevant related terms.

What is LSI keyword?

LSI (Latent Semantic Indexing) keywords are related terms and synonyms that reinforce the topic of your content, helping search engines understand context.
