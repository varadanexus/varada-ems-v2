FAQ

How is marketing ROI calculated?

ROI % = (revenue − marketing cost) ÷ marketing cost × 100. A positive figure means the campaign earned more than it cost.

What is the difference between ROI and ROAS?

ROAS = revenue ÷ ad spend (a ratio); ROI factors in that the spend itself is subtracted, expressed as a percentage of cost.

What is a good ROAS?

It depends on margins, but many businesses target a ROAS of 3–4× or higher to stay profitable after costs.
