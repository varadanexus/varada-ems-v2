FAQ

Which licence is needed first to open a hospital?

The Clinical Establishment registration and building/fire approvals are foundational; without these, no other state licences will be issued.

Is PCPNDT registration mandatory for all hospitals?

Yes, for any facility that performs ultrasound on pregnant women — including OPD ultrasound — PCPNDT registration is mandatory under law.

How long do hospital licences take in India?

Timelines vary by state: CEA registration can take 30–90 days; pollution consent 60–120 days; AERB and blood bank licences 3–6 months each.
