FAQ

What is a good training ROI?

Industry benchmark ROI for effective training programmes is 150–300%. Even a 10% productivity improvement on a ₹400,000 salary gives ₹40,000/year — often exceeding training cost.

How do I measure training effectiveness?

Use Kirkpatrick's 4 levels: Reaction (participant feedback), Learning (pre/post assessment), Behaviour (manager observation 30–90 days later), Results (KPI improvement).

What is the average training spend per employee in India?

Average L&D spend in India is ₹15,000–40,000 per employee per year. Technology companies and multinationals tend to invest significantly more.
