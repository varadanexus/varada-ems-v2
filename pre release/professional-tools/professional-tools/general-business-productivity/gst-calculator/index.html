FAQ

How is GST calculated on an amount?

To add GST, GST = amount × rate ÷ 100 and total = amount + GST. To remove GST from an inclusive price, base = amount × 100 ÷ (100 + rate).

What is the CGST and SGST split?

For intra-state sales the GST is shared equally: CGST = SGST = half the total GST. For inter-state sales the full amount is charged as IGST.

What are the GST slabs in India?

The common GST rates are 5%, 12%, 18% and 28%, with some goods at 0% or special rates.
