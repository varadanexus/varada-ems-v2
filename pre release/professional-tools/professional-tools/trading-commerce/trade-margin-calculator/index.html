FAQ

What is the difference between margin and markup?

Margin is profit as a % of selling price. Markup is profit as a % of cost. A 25% margin equals a 33.3% markup. Always clarify which metric buyers or accountants are referring to.

What is a healthy gross margin for traders?

Depends on industry: commodity trading 2–8%, branded goods 20–40%, specialty/niche products 40–60%. Net margin after all costs is typically 5–15% for healthy trading businesses.

How does GST affect trading margins?

GST is a pass-through for GST-registered traders (input credit available). For unregistered businesses or B2C with no ITC, GST directly reduces effective margin.
