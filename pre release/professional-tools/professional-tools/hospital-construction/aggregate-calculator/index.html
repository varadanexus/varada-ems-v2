FAQ

How much aggregate per m³ of concrete?

About 0.84 m³ (≈30 cft) of coarse aggregate per m³ of M20 concrete (1:1.5:3).

What size aggregate is used?

20 mm is common for general RCC; 10 mm for thin or congested sections.

Does this include wastage?

No — add roughly 3–5% for site wastage.
