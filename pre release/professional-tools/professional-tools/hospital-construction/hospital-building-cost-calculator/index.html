FAQ

What is the construction cost of a hospital per sq ft?

Civil work is typically ₹2,500–4,500/sq ft; with MEP, HVAC and services the all-in building cost is often ₹4,000–7,000/sq ft.

Does this include medical equipment?

No, this is building cost only. Equipment is usually an additional ₹5–15 lakh per bed.

What is contingency for?

A buffer (typically 5–10%) for design changes, price escalation and site conditions.
