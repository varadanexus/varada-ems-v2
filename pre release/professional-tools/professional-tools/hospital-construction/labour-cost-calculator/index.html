FAQ

How is construction labour cost calculated?

Multiply each crew’s headcount by its daily wage and the number of working days, then add the crews.

Are statutory costs included?

No — add PF, ESI, insurance and any overtime separately, or use a fully-loaded wage.

How do I control labour cost?

Improve productivity, sequence trades to avoid idle time and track daily output against plan.
