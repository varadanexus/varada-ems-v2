FAQ

What is a good IRR for a hospital project?

Typically 15–22% equity IRR is considered acceptable for Indian private hospitals; super-specialty tertiary hospitals can target higher returns given higher ARPOB.

What is ARPOB?

Average Revenue Per Occupied Bed per Day — a key hospital performance metric combining bed occupancy and revenue intensity.

How long to break even for a hospital?

Cash break-even typically 2–4 years; accounting/investment break-even 6–10 years for a greenfield hospital depending on scale and payer mix.
