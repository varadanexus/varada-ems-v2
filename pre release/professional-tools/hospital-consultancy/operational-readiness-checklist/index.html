FAQ

What is the most common gap at hospital launch?

HMS not fully tested, inadequate nursing staffing and pending BMW authorisation are the three most common gaps that delay launches.

Can a hospital do a soft launch before all licences are in place?

No — the Clinical Establishment licence and fire NOC are mandatory before any patient can be admitted, with no exceptions.

How long before opening should the readiness checklist be started?

Start 3–4 months before target opening; critical infrastructure and regulatory items should be tracked 6+ months in advance.
