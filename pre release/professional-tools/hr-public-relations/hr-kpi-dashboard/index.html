FAQ

What is a healthy attrition rate?

10–15% annual attrition is generally considered healthy for most industries in India. IT sector averages 20–25%. Below 5% may indicate lack of growth opportunities.

What is a good absenteeism rate?

Absenteeism below 1.5% per month (18% per year) is generally acceptable. Above 2% warrants investigation into employee wellness, engagement or management issues.

How do I improve revenue per employee?

Revenue per employee improves through automation, upskilling, better sales processes and reducing headcount in low-value activities. Benchmark varies widely by industry.
