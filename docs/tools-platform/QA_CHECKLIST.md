# Tools Platform QA Checklist

## Strategy QA

- Is the tool problem specific?
- Is the target user clear?
- Is the sector alignment clear?
- Is there a credible SEO keyword target?
- Is there a relevant service CTA?

## Logic QA

- Are formulas documented?
- Are assumptions explicit?
- Are rules deterministic?
- Are edge cases identified?
- Is the output understandable?

## Content QA

- Is the title clear?
- Is the page introduction concise?
- Are FAQs relevant?
- Are disclaimers accurate?
- Is there unnecessary jargon?

## SEO QA

- Is there one clear primary keyword intent?
- Are headings structured well?
- Is metadata defined?
- Is internal linking planned?
- Is the CTA aligned with search intent?

## Conversion QA

- Does the result naturally lead to a service need?
- Is the CTA specific?
- Is lead capture minimal but useful?
- Is the offer matched to the result context?

## Final Gate

- Ready for specification
- Ready for design
- Ready for implementation
- Needs revision
